# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors are expected to communicate clearly, respectfully, and in good faith.
Technical disagreement is normal. Personal attacks, harassment, intimidation, and exclusionary behavior are not acceptable.

## Expected Behavior

- be respectful in discussions and reviews
- focus criticism on ideas, code, and design decisions
- assume good intent unless behavior shows otherwise
- help keep the project usable for contributors with different experience levels

## Unacceptable Behavior

- personal attacks or insults
- discriminatory or demeaning language
- harassment in public or private channels
- doxxing, threats, or intimidation
- intentionally disruptive behavior that blocks collaboration

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, review comments, and other project communication spaces.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and discussions that violate these standards.
Serious or repeated violations may lead to temporary or permanent exclusion from the project space.
